About Me

Hello! My name is Alfred Downmount Simaremare, S.Mat., and you can call me Al. I am a Mathematics graduate from Institut Teknologi Sumatera (ITERA) with a strong passion for Data Analytics and data-driven problem solving. For me, mathematics is not only about numbers and formulas, but also about how data can be transformed into meaningful insights that support better and smarter decision-making. During my academic journey, I studied various areas including statistics, machine learning, forecasting, mathematical modeling, data analysis, and numerical computation. I am also experienced in using several analytical and data processing tools such as Python, R, MATLAB, Microsoft Excel, and other data management systems to analyze and interpret data effectively. Professionally, I worked as a Tutorial Assistant at Institut Teknologi Sumatera for approximately three years (2023–2026), assisting students in various mathematics-related courses and helping them understand analytical and problem-solving concepts more effectively. This experience strengthened my communication skills, structured thinking, and ability to explain complex information clearly. In addition, I completed a three-month internship at PT Angkasa Pura Indonesia, where I was assigned to three different departments: Airport Commercial, Airport Quality & Safety Management System (AQSMS), and Asset Management & General. During the internship, I became familiar with operational data processing and analysis using the company’s internal system called OASYS to support reporting, monitoring, and data-driven decision-making processes. I am known as a detail-oriented, disciplined, and highly committed individual, especially when it comes to work quality and data processing accuracy. I genuinely enjoy working with data because I believe every dataset contains valuable stories and insights that can lead to more effective, efficient, and strategic decisions. With a strong mathematical background, hands-on analytical experience, professional exposure, and communication skills, I am eager to continue growing and contributing in the field of Data Analytics and other data-driven industries.
